Brevet Day Checklist

Must-Have on the Bike: Front light (1000+ lumens), tail light, reflective vest, navigation device, portable charger, saddle/TT/frame bag

Tire Kit (Tubed): Extra tubes (2), CO2 cartridges (2), mini pump, patch kit, tire levers, tire boot

Tire Kit (Tubeless): All of the above plus dynaplug kit, valve core removal tool, spare sealant

Tools: Multi-tool with chain breaker, spare master links (2 pairs), chain lube, spare AXS battery (if applicable)

Nutrition: Gels, bars, salt tabs — plan for self-sufficiency between controls

Post-Ride: Protein shake, towel, change of clothes, extra water, comfort food
